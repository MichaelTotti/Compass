BNB (Binance Coin) is a cryptocurrency originally launched by the Binance exchange in 2017. Initially created as a utility token to pay for transaction fees on the Binance platform, BNB has evolved and can now be used for a variety of purposes, including trading, staking, and participating in token sales. BNB operates on Binance's own blockchain, Binance Chain, and has gained significant popularity due to its widespread use within the Binance ecosystem and beyond.
