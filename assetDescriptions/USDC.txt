USD Coin (USDC) is a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US dollar, issued by Circle and backed by fully audited reserves of cash and short-term US Treasury bonds. It operates on multiple blockchains, including Ethereum, Solana, and Polygon, making it widely used in DeFi, payments, and remittances. USDC is known for its transparency and regulatory compliance, making it a trusted choice for stable digital transactions.
