Tether (USDT) is a stablecoin designed to maintain a 1:1 value with the US dollar. It is widely used in the crypto market for trading, remittances, and as a hedge against volatility. USDT is issued by Tether Limited and operates on multiple blockchains, including Ethereum, Tron, and Solana. While it provides liquidity and stability, concerns exist regarding its reserves and transparency.
